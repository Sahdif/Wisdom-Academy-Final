Tajweed

What is Tajweed?

Tajweed is a set of rules that govern the proper pronunciation and articulation of the Arabic letters and words in the Quran. These rules are based on the pronunciation of the Prophet Muhammad (Peace Be Upon Him) and are designed to ensure that the Quran is recited correctly and beautifully.

Tajweed (Juz Amma & Fatiha)

Your teacher who is skilled in teaching these rules and can help you to learn how to properly recite Juz Amma and Surah Fatiha. There are a variety of teaching methods, including audio and visual aids, to ensure that you fully understand the rules of tajweed.
